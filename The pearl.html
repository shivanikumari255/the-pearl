Information about hotel

 A hotel is a building that offers short-term lodging, meals, and other services to travelers. Hotels can be large buildings with many rooms, or smaller establishments like bed and breakfasts. What do hotels offer? Rooms: Hotels offer private rooms with en-suite bathrooms. Rooms can range from small rooms with a mattress to large suites with kitchen facilities, upholstered chairs, and televisions. Meals: Hotels may offer meals and other services. Conferences: Some hotels are used for conferences. What are some types of hotels? Motels: Small, low-rise hotels with direct access to rooms from the parking lot Inns: Smaller hotels that have been around since ancient times Extended stay hotels: Hotels where suites are rented by the week Bed and breakfasts: Smaller establishments that offer private rooms and breakfast How are hotels run? Hotels are run by a general manager, department heads, middle managers, administrative staff, and line-level supervisors.
